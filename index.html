A scholarship is an award of financial aid for a student to further their education. Scholarships are awarded based upon various criteria, which usually reflect the values and purposes of the donor or founder of the award. Scholarship money is not required to be repaid. This application is about helping you rank schools and seeing which one offers the best scholarships for you & and the school that best suits you.
